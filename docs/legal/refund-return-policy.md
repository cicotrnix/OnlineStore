# Refund & Return Policy (B2B)

This policy applies to wholesale purchases from **KonLLC dba PiPower** and works together with the Limited Warranty in our Terms of Sale.

## 1. RMA required
All returns require a Return Merchandise Authorization (RMA) **before** shipping anything back. Request one at **support@pipower.shop** with your order number and reason. Unauthorized returns may be refused or not credited.

## 2. Lithium batteries — return shipping is restricted
Lithium‑ion products are regulated dangerous goods. **Do not ship batteries back in ordinary packaging or mixed with other goods.** Returns must follow the hazmat packaging and labeling instructions we provide with the RMA. We may decline returns that arrive non‑compliant, damaged, swollen, or installed/used, for safety and regulatory reasons.

## 3. Limited warranty — defective products
Products with a manufacturing defect within the **12‑month** warranty window are eligible for replacement or credit after inspection. Report suspected defects within **30 days** of discovery. We may require photos, the battery health/diagnostic readout, and the unit's identifiers before issuing an RMA. The warranty excludes damage from improper installation, mishandling, abuse, water, or physical damage.

## 4. Buyer's‑remorse / ordering errors
Unopened, unused product in original packaging may be returnable within **30 days**, subject to a restocking fee of **15%** and Buyer‑paid compliant return shipping. Opened, installed, or used batteries are **not** returnable except under warranty.

## 5. Non‑returnable
Installed/used cells; damaged, swollen, or punctured cells; items outside the return window; clearance/final‑sale items; products not purchased from us.

## 6. Inspection and outcome
Returns are inspected on receipt. Approved returns are resolved by replacement, store credit, or refund at our discretion. Refunds are issued to the original payment method (card refund via Stripe, or wire reversal to the originating account) within **5–10 business days** of approval.

## 7. Shipping costs
Outbound and return shipping for buyer's‑remorse returns are non‑refundable and paid by Buyer. For confirmed defects, we cover compliant return shipping per the RMA instructions.
