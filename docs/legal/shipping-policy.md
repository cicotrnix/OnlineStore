Shipping for wholesale purchases from **KonLLC dba PiPower**.

## 1. Where we ship
We ship within the United States via FedEx Ground. International orders are handled as exports through a freight forwarder (Miami); see §5.

## 2. Lithium battery (dangerous goods) handling
Our products are lithium‑ion (UN3480/UN3481, Class 9) and ship under dangerous‑goods rules with compliant packaging and labeling. This may affect available service levels, add a hazmat handling fee, and require ground transport. Orders cannot ship to P.O. boxes or by air unless specifically supported.

## 3. Processing and handling time
Orders ship within **1–2 business days** of confirmed payment. Dangerous‑goods preparation may add handling time. You receive tracking when the label is created.

## 4. Rates, delivery, and signature
Shipping is calculated at checkout or quoted on the invoice. Delivery estimates are not guaranteed. Signature may be required. **Risk of loss passes on delivery to the carrier (FOB origin)**; claims for transit damage or loss are filed with the carrier — keep all packaging and notify us within **2 business days**.

## 5. International / export
For non‑US destinations, the **Buyer is the importer of record** and is responsible for all duties, taxes, customs clearance, and compliance with destination import and dangerous‑goods rules. Export shipments are arranged through our forwarder; transit times and costs vary and are quoted separately.

## 6. Address accuracy and undeliverable shipments
You are responsible for a correct, complete shipping address. Reshipment of packages returned as undeliverable due to an incorrect address may incur additional charges.
