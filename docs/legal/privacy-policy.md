# Privacy Policy

> **Draft for legal review — not legal advice.** Have a licensed attorney review and finalize before publishing. Items in `[BRACKETS]` must be completed. Last updated: [FECHA].

**KonLLC dba PiPower** ("PiPower," "we," "us") operates this wholesale store. This policy explains what business and personal information we collect, how we use it, and your rights. This is a B2B service not directed to consumers or to children under 16.

## 1. Information we collect
- **Account & organization:** name, business name, email, role, addresses, and account activity.
- **Verification:** business tax ID, resale/exemption certificates you upload, and verification status.
- **Orders & payments:** order history and invoices. **Card payments are processed by Stripe; we do not store full card numbers.** Wire/ACH is reconciled from bank reference data you provide.
- **Support & chat:** messages you send, including to the AI assistant.
- **Technical:** IP address, device/browser data, cookies, and usage analytics.

## 2. How we use it
To create and manage your account, verify eligibility and tax status, process and ship orders, provide support and the AI assistant, prevent fraud and abuse, comply with legal and tax obligations, and improve the service.

## 3. Service providers (processors)
We share data only as needed with providers acting on our behalf, including: hosting (Hetzner/Coolify), email (Resend), payments (Stripe), search (Meilisearch, Voyage AI), AI assistant (Anthropic), analytics (PostHog, Google Analytics) when enabled, file storage (Cloudflare R2), and shipping/logistics (FedEx and freight forwarders). Each processes data under its own terms. **We do not sell your personal information.**

## 4. AI assistant
If you use the chat assistant, your messages are sent to our AI provider (Anthropic) to generate responses. Do not submit sensitive personal data in chat. Wholesale pricing in chat is shown only to verified businesses.

## 5. Retention
We keep information as long as your account is active and as required for tax, accounting, warranty, and legal purposes, then delete or anonymize it.

## 6. Security
We use access controls, encryption in transit, and an append‑only financial ledger. No method is perfectly secure; we cannot guarantee absolute security.

## 7. Your rights
Depending on your location (e.g., California CCPA/CPRA), you may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal information, and opt out of certain sharing. We do not discriminate for exercising these rights. To make a request, contact **privacy@pipower.shop**. [If you serve EU/UK customers, add GDPR/UK‑GDPR bases and rights with counsel.]

## 8. Cookies
We use cookies necessary for sign‑in, session, and preferences (language, catalog view) and, when enabled, analytics cookies. You can control cookies in your browser; disabling essential cookies may break sign‑in.

## 9. Changes
We may update this policy and will post the new date above.
